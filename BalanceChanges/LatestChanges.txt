SHUHEI Changes(by efra):


Base & Awakening:

Back Step Cancel Timing has been reduced from 52 -> 42
Notes: Shuhei is all about zoning with his buttons but due to the slower back step cancel compared to the rest of the cast, he could not zone effectively. He would be forced to stand still at medium ranges due to being punished from moving very easily. To make his movement less punishing as well as increase his zoning potential, this change was made to speed up his back dash.

Heavy Tilt guard stun from hit 2 has been increased from 20 -> 24
Notes: This change was made so shuhei is able to block after this move is blocked. He would previously get punished for free with many different buttons and moves. This change gives shuhei more advantage without it becoming problematic.

Light 3 guard damage has been reduced from 300 -> 150
Notes: Guard damage and guard break setups are a big problem on some characters and shuhei is one of them. He uses a mix of all of his buttons to get guaranteed hakugeki break setups to take free health stocks. To avoid this, guard damage was reduced to mitigate this style of play.

Sig cancel timing was reduced from 52 -> 32
Sig hit and guard stun was increased. (Base: Hit Stun 46 -> 47, Guard Stun 38 -> 47. Awakening: Hit Stun 36 -> 47, Guard Stun 28 -> 47)
Notes: Shuhei's sig both during hit and on block was negative enough to be freely punished by whatever move an opponent desired. Not only does this make this move one of the worst moves in the game, it made this button useless for shuhei. Cancel timing was reduced due to coding of directly being tied to cancel recovery. To not make shuhei very plus off this new cancel timing, adjusting hit and guard stun was necessary. With both changes together, shuhei is no longer punishable on hit or block but is also not plus enough to press any button or step in any direction.

SP1 (0-5 fear in base/ 3-8 fear in awakening [White Fear]) fighting spirit gain has been decreased from 0.3 -> 0
Notes: This change was done to fix a bug where sometimes in neutral shuhei would get and give a lot more fighting spirit than intended. To mitigate this bug from happening and considering current universal fighting spirit changes, this was the best course of action.
